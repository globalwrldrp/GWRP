Global Wrld Roleplay Rules and Regulations

  • Article 2.1 - Promotion Requests - Do not solicit promotions from staff members in any manner. Violation of this rule will result in disciplinary action.
  • Article 2.2 - Personal Information - Sharing personal information, including that of others, is strictly prohibited. You may share your own photos only in designated channels.
  • Article 2.3 - Unauthorized Server Links - Sending unsolicited links to other Discord servers via private messages will result in an immediate ban.
  • Article 2.4 - Server Advertisement - Posting links to other Discord servers in Global Wrld Roleplay chats will result in an immediate ban.
  • Article 2.5 - Respect and Conduct - Any form of disrespect, whether in voice channels or text channels, is strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated.
  • Article 2.6 - Impersonation - Do not impersonate staff members under any circumstances. Violations will result in severe penalties.
  • Article 2.7 - Prohibited Content - Sexual remarks, content, and NSFW materials are strictly forbidden. This includes images, speech, and videos.
  • Article 2.8 - Language Use - English is the primary language for communication in Global Wrld Roleplay. Roleplay scenarios may use other languages as required by the roleplay context.
  • Article 2.9 - Threats and Harassment - Any form of threats, including death threats or other forms of harassment, will result in an immediate blacklist ban.
  • Article 2.10 - Account Limitations - Each member is allowed only one Discord account within the server. Additional accounts (alts) will be removed, and the user will face punishment.
  • Article 2.11 - Management Decisions - All decisions made by the Management team are final and non-negotiable.
  • Article 2.12 - Offensive Content - Posting offensive, inflammatory, or discriminatory content will result in immediate disciplinary action, including possible ban.
  • Article 2.13 - Spamming - Spamming in any channel is prohibited. This includes repetitive messages, excessive use of emojis, and irrelevant content.
  • Article 2.14 - Bot Usage - Unauthorized use of bots is not allowed. Only approved bots may be used within the server.
  • Article 2.15 - Reporting Violations - Report any rule violations to the staff immediately. False reporting may result in penalties.
  • Article 2.16 - Privacy and DMs - Respect others' privacy. Do not send unsolicited direct messages (DMs) to other members without their consent.
  • Article 2.17 - Event Participation - Follow all specific rules and guidelines set forth for events and activities. Failure to comply may result in removal from the event and additional consequences.
  • Article 2.18 - Roleplay Conduct - Maintain a respectful and immersive roleplay environment. Meta-gaming, power-gaming, and breaking character without valid reason are not allowed.
  • Article 2.19 - Use of Media - All media (images, videos, etc.) shared must adhere to server rules and not contain any prohibited content.
  • Article 2.20 - Confidentiality - Respect the confidentiality of server-related discussions and plans. Unauthorized disclosure of private information is prohibited.
  • Article 2.21 - Moderation Assistance - Do not attempt to moderate or enforce rules unless you are a designated staff member. Report issues to staff instead.

By participating in Global Wrld Roleplay, you agree to abide by these rules. Failure to adhere to these regulations will result in appropriate disciplinary actions determined by the Management team.
